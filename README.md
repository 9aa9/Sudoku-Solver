# Sudoku-Solver
Python script for solving 9x9 sudoku puzzles

Enter the sudoku puzzle as shown in the example below:

Real Puzzle

7 2 _ 1 _ _ 8 _ _
_ _ _ _ _ 6 3 _ _
_ 9 _ 3 _ 7 _ _ 5
_ _ _ _ 6 _ _ _ _
4 _ _ 9 _ 1 _ 6 _
_ _ 5 7 _ _ _ _ _
_ _ 1 _ _ 9 _ 3 2
2 _ _ _ 3 _ 4 _ 1
